Explorations & Spikes

Experiential spikes probing a visual-novel learning game built around the ENGRAM papers (ALMA & AMA-Bench). Each spike is a runnable, zero-dependency demo plus a writeup. Open a play demo to feel it; read the writeup for the investigation and verdict.

SPIKE 001 · VALIDATED

vn engine feel

Given the existing manga panels + ENGRAM//KIT brand, when a player clicks through a VN scene (panel background, name plate, typewriter dialogue, click-to-advance, branching choice), then it feels like a real visual novel and runs on the static site with zero build.
